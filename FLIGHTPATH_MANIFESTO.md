# FLIGHTPATH: Multidimensional Logic & Vector Intersections (V1.0)
**Project Codename**: The Zenith Wedge
**Mission**: Building a system for AI to "see" and navigate across disparate logic fields through vector intersection.

## 01. The Theory of Logic Planes
Knowledge is not linear; it is planar.
- **Plane S (Sovereignty)**: Governed by the laws of the State and the Authority Root.
- **Plane C (Commerciality)**: Governed by the laws of the Market and the Lead Cycle.
- **Plane Q (Quantum)**: Governed by the internal logic of the "One" and the Sovereign Engine.

## 02. The Intersection Point (The Solve)
In 3D Vector Space, three planes intersect at a single **POINT**. 
In the Flightpath Engine, the AI iterates through these planes to find the "Zenith Point"—the only strategic move that is simultaneously:
1. Legally Sovereign
2. Commercially Viable 
3. Technically Quantum

## 03. The Flightpath Engine (Program Logic)
- **Layer 1: Domain Encoding**: Transform disparate data (CSVs, Whitepapers, Resumes) into a weighted Vector Space.
- **Layer 2: Plane Modeling**: Define the "Normal Vectors" for each logic field (e.g., "Privacy" is the normal for the Sovereign Plane).
- **Layer 3: Intersection Calculation**: Use Linear Algebra (SVD or Plane-Plane Intersection) to find the "Strategic Trajectory."

## 04. Tactical Application
The "Flightpath" is the resulting script or roadmap that takes an Agent from A to B through the safest, most valid logic intersections.
